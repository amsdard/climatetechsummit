Climate tech business in Benelux awards

Top 100 most impactful Climate Tech Businesses in Benelux awards

In collaboration with MT/sprout & Change.inc, we will, for the first time, nominate the most impactful climate-tech business in the Benelux region.

The event will feature a Grand Winner and six category-specific winners. Nominations will be determined through a combination of public voting and evaluations by a panel of expert judges. Participants will have the opportunity to nominate businesses, share content, and engage on social media.

“Our mission" is to generate and sustain traction, driving impactful businesses that are essential for solving climate challenges.

We are uniting the Benelux Climate Tech ecosystem by connecting founders and financial professionals to accelerate growth and maximize impact. By sharing best practices, failures, and successes, we aim to inspire founders and build a strong community. The path is tough, but together, we can move forward.
